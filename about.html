OUR COMPANY

Established in the year 2014, at Gurugram, Haryana (India), we “OM Industries,” are one of the leading companies engaged in meeting the manufacturing demands of special purpose machines, Assembly lines for different applications, and different segments for our customers. We are Specialized in Leak Testing Machine dry & wet both. A Proprietorship Firm, with branch offices at Gurugram, we started with the manufacturing of Tool Room Activities.

Gradually with developed process expertise, we expanded our business in the manufacturing of complete assembly lines as well as Special Purpose Machine’s inclusive very effective Automation Solutions with an increase in productivity of any system, decreasing there manpower dependency, increasing in accuracy and error-proofing projects that included handling the design, installation, and commissioning. Our business operations are led by Mr. Jagjit Singh Tehlan. His experienced business approach as well as experienced service support has helped us to emerge as pioneers in the field of specialized manufacturing to all types of SPM and its installation.
